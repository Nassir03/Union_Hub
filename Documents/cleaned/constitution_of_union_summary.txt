Title: Constitution of the Union Summary
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

The Constitution of the United Republic of Tanzania is central to understanding the Union. It defines the structure of the United Republic, Union authority, rights and duties, national institutions, and the constitutional relationship between Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.

The Constitution is important for students because it changes Union learning from memory of dates into understanding of institutions, responsibilities, rights, and governance.

Katiba ya Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania ni muhimu katika kuelewa Muungano kwa sababu inaeleza muundo wa Jamhuri ya Muungano, mamlaka, haki, wajibu, taasisi na uhusiano wa kikatiba kati ya Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar.
