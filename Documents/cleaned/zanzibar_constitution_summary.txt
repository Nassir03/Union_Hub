Title: Zanzibar Constitution Summary
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

The Constitution of Zanzibar explains the structure of the Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar and Zanzibar institutions. It is important in Union education because it helps learners understand Zanzibar's internal government within the wider framework of the United Republic.

Understanding the Zanzibar Constitution helps users avoid confusing Union matters with non-Union matters. It also helps students understand how Zanzibar's institutions relate to the Union framework.

Katiba ya Zanzibar inaeleza muundo wa Serikali ya Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar na taasisi za Zanzibar. Ni muhimu katika elimu ya Muungano kwa sababu husaidia kuelewa nafasi ya Zanzibar ndani ya Jamhuri ya Muungano.
