Title: Articles of Union Summary
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

The Articles of Union are the founding agreement that connected Tanganyika and Zanzibar into one United Republic. They were signed by Julius Kambarage Nyerere and Abeid Amani Karume on 22 April 1964. The United Republic was formed on 26 April 1964.

The Articles of Union are important because they provide the legal and political foundation for the Union. They are a key reference when learning why the Union exists, who founded it, and how Union matters became part of Tanzania's constitutional framework.
