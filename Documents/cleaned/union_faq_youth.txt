Source URL: local curated FAQ
Source Type: faq

Question: How does the Union help young people?
Answer: The Union helps young people by strengthening national identity, creating a wider civic space, supporting movement and interaction, and connecting education, culture, business, and public institutions.

Question: Muungano unawasaidiaje vijana?
Answer: Muungano huwasaidia vijana kwa kuimarisha utambulisho wa taifa, kuweka nafasi pana ya ushiriki wa kiraia, kusaidia mwingiliano wa watu, na kuunganisha elimu, utamaduni, biashara na taasisi za umma.

Question: Why should youth care about the Union?
Answer: Youth should care because the Union affects their identity, opportunities, civic responsibility, and understanding of Tanzania's national institutions.
