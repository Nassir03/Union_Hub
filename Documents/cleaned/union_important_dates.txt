Title: Union Important Dates
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

Important dates for Union education include 9 December 1961, when Tanganyika became independent; 9 December 1962, when Tanganyika became a republic; 12 January 1964, when the Zanzibar Revolution took place; 22 April 1964, when the Articles of Union were signed; and 26 April 1964, when the United Republic was formed.

Tarehe muhimu ni 9 Desemba 1961 Tanganyika ilipopata uhuru, 9 Desemba 1962 Tanganyika ilipokuwa jamhuri, 12 Januari 1964 Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar, 22 Aprili 1964 kutiwa saini Hati za Muungano, na 26 Aprili 1964 kuundwa kwa Jamhuri ya Muungano.
