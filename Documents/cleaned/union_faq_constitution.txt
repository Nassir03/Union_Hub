Question: Why is the Constitution important to the Union?
Answer: The Constitution explains the structure of the United Republic, Union powers, institutions, rights, duties, and the relationship between Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.

Question: Kwa nini Katiba ni muhimu kwa Muungano?
Answer: Katiba inaeleza muundo wa Jamhuri ya Muungano, mamlaka ya Muungano, taasisi, haki, wajibu na uhusiano kati ya Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar.

Question: What are Union matters?
Answer: Union matters are areas handled at the level of the United Republic for both Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar under the constitutional framework.
