Title: Tanzania and Zanzibar Leaders Around the Union
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

Julius Kambarage Nyerere was President of Tanganyika before the Union and became the first President of the United Republic after the Union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar.

Abeid Amani Karume was President of Zanzibar and Chairman of the Revolutionary Council after the Zanzibar Revolution. He was one of the founders of the Union and became Vice-President of the United Republic.

Rashid Mfaume Kawawa was an important Tanganyika and Union leader who helped in early nation-building and government administration.

Julius Kambarage Nyerere alikuwa Rais wa Tanganyika kabla ya Muungano na akawa Rais wa kwanza wa Jamhuri ya Muungano baada ya Muungano wa Tanganyika na Zanzibar.

Abeid Amani Karume alikuwa Rais wa Zanzibar na Mwenyekiti wa Baraza la Mapinduzi baada ya Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar. Alikuwa mmoja wa waasisi wa Muungano na akawa Makamu wa Rais wa Jamhuri ya Muungano.

Rashid Mfaume Kawawa alikuwa kiongozi muhimu wa Tanganyika na Muungano aliyesaidia katika hatua za awali za ujenzi wa taifa na utawala.
