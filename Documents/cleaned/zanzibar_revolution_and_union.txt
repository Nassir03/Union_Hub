Title: Zanzibar Revolution and the Union
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

The Zanzibar Revolution took place on 12 January 1964. It overthrew the Sultanate and led to a revolutionary government led by Abeid Amani Karume. This political change happened shortly before the Union with Tanganyika.

The Revolution is important in Union education because it explains the political context in Zanzibar before the Articles of Union were signed. It helps learners understand why Zanzibar's leadership after January 1964 became central to the Union process.

Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar yalifanyika tarehe 12 Januari 1964. Yaliuangusha Usultani na kuanzisha Serikali ya Mapinduzi iliyoongozwa na Abeid Amani Karume. Tukio hili ni muhimu kuelewa mazingira ya Muungano.
