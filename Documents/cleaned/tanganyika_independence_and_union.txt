Title: Tanganyika Independence and the Union
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

Tanganyika became independent on 9 December 1961. Julius Kambarage Nyerere became Prime Minister after independence. Tanganyika became a republic on 9 December 1962, and Nyerere became President.

This background is important because Tanganyika entered the Union as an independent republic led by President Nyerere. Understanding Tanganyika before 1964 helps learners answer questions about leadership before the Union.
