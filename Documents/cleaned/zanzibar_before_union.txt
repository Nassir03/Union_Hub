Title: Zanzibar Before the Union
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

Before the Union in April 1964, Zanzibar went through major political change. Zanzibar gained independence from British protection in December 1963 under a Sultanate. The Zanzibar Revolution took place on 12 January 1964 and overthrew the Sultanate.

After the Revolution, Abeid Amani Karume became President of Zanzibar and Chairman of the Revolutionary Council. This revolutionary government later entered into the Union with Tanganyika.

Kabla ya Muungano wa Aprili 1964, Zanzibar ilipitia mabadiliko makubwa ya kisiasa. Zanzibar ilipata uhuru kutoka chini ya ulinzi wa Uingereza mwezi Desemba 1963 ikiwa chini ya Usultani. Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar yalifanyika tarehe 12 Januari 1964 na kuuangusha Usultani.

Baada ya Mapinduzi, Abeid Amani Karume alikuwa Rais wa Zanzibar na Mwenyekiti wa Baraza la Mapinduzi. Serikali hii ya Mapinduzi ndiyo baadaye iliingia katika Muungano na Tanganyika.
