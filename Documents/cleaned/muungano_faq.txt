Title: Muungano Frequently Asked Questions
Source URL: local curated FAQ
Source Type: faq

Question: Muungano ni nini?
Answer: Muungano ni makubaliano ya Tanganyika na Zanzibar kuunda Jamhuri ya Muungano yenye mamlaka ya pamoja katika mambo ya Muungano.

Question: Muungano ulianzishwa lini?
Answer: Muungano ulianzishwa rasmi tarehe 26 Aprili 1964 baada ya Hati za Muungano kusainiwa tarehe 22 Aprili 1964.

Question: Nani alitawala Tanganyika kabla ya Muungano?
Answer: Kabla tu ya Muungano wa Aprili 1964, Tanganyika ilikuwa jamhuri iliyoongozwa na Rais Julius Kambarage Nyerere. Kabla ya hapo, Tanganyika ilipata uhuru tarehe 9 Desemba 1961, na Nyerere alikuwa Waziri Mkuu; ilipokuwa jamhuri tarehe 9 Desemba 1962, Nyerere akawa Rais.

Question: Who ruled Tanganyika before the Union?
Answer: Immediately before the Union in April 1964, Tanganyika was a republic led by President Julius Kambarage Nyerere. Tanganyika became independent on 9 December 1961, and Nyerere became Prime Minister; when Tanganyika became a republic on 9 December 1962, Nyerere became President.

Question: Nani alitawala Zanzibar kabla ya Muungano?
Answer: Kabla ya Muungano, Zanzibar ilikuwa imepinduliwa tarehe 12 Januari 1964. Baada ya Mapinduzi, Abeid Amani Karume alikuwa Rais wa Zanzibar na Mwenyekiti wa Baraza la Mapinduzi. Kabla ya Mapinduzi, Zanzibar ilikuwa chini ya Usultani.

Question: Who ruled Zanzibar before the Union?
Answer: Before the Union, Zanzibar experienced the Revolution of 12 January 1964. After the Revolution, Abeid Amani Karume became President of Zanzibar and Chairman of the Revolutionary Council. Before the Revolution, Zanzibar was under the Sultanate.

Question: Kwa nini vijana wajali Muungano?
Answer: Vijana wajali Muungano kwa sababu unaathiri utambulisho wa taifa, elimu, ajira, biashara, uhamaji, utamaduni na ushiriki wao katika masuala ya kitaifa.

Question: How does the Union help students?
Answer: The Union helps students by strengthening shared national identity, access to national institutions, understanding of constitutional history, and education opportunities between Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.
