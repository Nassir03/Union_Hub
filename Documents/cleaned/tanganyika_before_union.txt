Title: Tanganyika Before the Union
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

Before the Union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar in 1964, Tanganyika was led by Mwalimu Julius Kambarage Nyerere.

Tanganyika became independent from British rule on 9 December 1961. After independence, Julius Kambarage Nyerere became Prime Minister of Tanganyika. Tanganyika became a republic on 9 December 1962, and Nyerere became President of Tanganyika.

Therefore, immediately before the Union with Zanzibar in April 1964, Tanganyika was governed as a republic under President Julius Kambarage Nyerere.

Kabla ya Muungano wa Tanganyika na Zanzibar mwaka 1964, Tanganyika iliongozwa na Mwalimu Julius Kambarage Nyerere.

Tanganyika ilipata uhuru kutoka kwa utawala wa Uingereza tarehe 9 Desemba 1961. Baada ya uhuru, Julius Kambarage Nyerere alikuwa Waziri Mkuu wa Tanganyika. Tanganyika ilipokuwa jamhuri tarehe 9 Desemba 1962, Nyerere akawa Rais wa Tanganyika.

Kwa hiyo, kabla tu ya Muungano na Zanzibar mwezi Aprili 1964, Tanganyika ilikuwa jamhuri iliyokuwa chini ya Rais Julius Kambarage Nyerere.
