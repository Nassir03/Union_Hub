Title: Parliament of Tanzania Constitution Reference
Source URL: https://www.parliament.go.tz/documents/constitution-of-tanzania
Source Type: official parliament document page

This note records an official reference point for the Constitution of the United Republic of Tanzania.

Key facts:
- The Parliament of Tanzania hosts the Constitution of Tanzania document page.
- The Constitution of the United Republic of Tanzania of 1977 is the key constitutional reference for the Union Government, Parliament, rights and duties, public authority, courts, finance, defence, and other national institutions.
- The First Schedule of the Constitution contains Union matters.
- The Constitution is important for understanding the legal structure of the United Republic and the relationship between Union institutions and Zanzibar institutions.

Learning use:
This source supports questions about the 1977 Constitution, Union institutions, Parliament, constitutional duties, and the legal basis of Union matters.
