Title: Official Tanzania Government Union Matters Summary
Source URL: https://www.tanzania.go.tz/index.php/topics/union-matters
Source Type: official government web page

This note summarizes official public education points from the Tanzania Government Portal page on Muungano.

Key facts:
- The United Republic of Tanzania was formed on 26 April 1964 through the Union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar.
- Julius Kambarage Nyerere became the first President of the United Republic.
- Abeid Amani Karume became First Vice President of the United Republic, President of Zanzibar, and Chairman of the Revolutionary Council.
- Rashidi Mfaume Kawawa served as Second Vice President and leader of government business in Parliament.
- The Articles of Union were signed on 22 April 1964.
- On 26 April 1964, the Tanganyika Parliament passed Union laws and Zanzibar's Revolutionary Council confirmed them.
- On 27 April 1964, the founders exchanged the Articles of Union and several members from Zanzibar were sworn into the Union Parliament.
- The 1977 Constitution of the United Republic and the 1984 Constitution of Zanzibar are rooted in the 1964 Articles of Union.
- The official page lists reasons for the Union including historical closeness, blood relations, trade, culture, Swahili language, political cooperation between TANU and ASP, and wider African unity aspirations.
- The official page also teaches the 22 Union matters, including the Constitution and Union Government, foreign affairs, defence and security, police, citizenship, immigration, higher education, statistics, weather, the Court of Appeal, and registration of political parties.

Learning use:
This source is useful for quiz questions about dates, founders, Union reasons, the Articles of Union, constitutional foundations, and the 22 Union matters.
