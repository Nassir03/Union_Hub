Title: Founders of the Union Summary
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

The two leaders most directly associated with the founding of the Union are Julius Kambarage Nyerere of Tanganyika and Abeid Amani Karume of Zanzibar. They signed the Articles of Union in April 1964.

Nyerere was President of Tanganyika before the Union and became the first President of the United Republic. Karume was President of Zanzibar and Chairman of the Revolutionary Council after the Zanzibar Revolution and became Vice-President of the United Republic.

Viongozi waasisi wanaohusishwa moja kwa moja na Muungano ni Julius Kambarage Nyerere wa Tanganyika na Abeid Amani Karume wa Zanzibar. Walitia saini Hati za Muungano mwezi Aprili 1964.
