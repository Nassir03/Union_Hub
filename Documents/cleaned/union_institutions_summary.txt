Title: Union Institutions Summary
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

Union institutions are the structures that help administer matters belonging to the United Republic. They include national organs connected to the Constitution, Parliament, foreign affairs, defence and security, police, citizenship, immigration, taxation and customs, higher education, statistics, and other Union matters.

Learning about Union institutions helps citizens understand which matters are handled by the Union Government and which matters are handled by the Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar or other authorities.
