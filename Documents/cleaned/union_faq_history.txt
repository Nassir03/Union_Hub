Question: When was the Union formed?
Answer: The Articles of Union were signed on 22 April 1964, and the United Republic was formed on 26 April 1964.

Question: Muungano ulianzishwa lini?
Answer: Hati za Muungano zilisainiwa tarehe 22 Aprili 1964, na Jamhuri ya Muungano iliundwa tarehe 26 Aprili 1964.

Question: Who founded the Union?
Answer: Julius Kambarage Nyerere and Abeid Amani Karume are the two leaders most directly associated with founding the Union.
