Title: Constitution Notes for Union Education
Source URL: local curated education note
Source Type: curated

The Constitution of the United Republic of Tanzania provides the main constitutional framework for the Union. It explains the structure of the United Republic, Union matters, rights, duties, and the authority of Union institutions.

The Constitution of Zanzibar explains the structure of the Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar and Zanzibar institutions within the wider United Republic framework.

Katiba ya Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania ndiyo msingi mkuu wa kikatiba wa Muungano. Inaeleza muundo wa Jamhuri ya Muungano, mambo ya Muungano, haki, wajibu na mamlaka ya taasisi za Muungano.

Katiba ya Zanzibar inaeleza muundo wa Serikali ya Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar na taasisi za Zanzibar ndani ya mfumo mpana wa Jamhuri ya Muungano.
