MUUNDO WA MUUNGANO — MFUMO WA SERIKALI MBILI

Muungano wa Tanzania una muundo wa pekee wa serikali mbili — tofauti na
nchi nyingi za kawaida zenye serikali moja. Muundo huu unaitwa "Two-
Government Structure" kwa Kiingereza.

SERIKALI YA KWANZA: SERIKALI YA JAMHURI YA MUUNGANO

Serikali ya Muungano inasimamia mambo yanayoathiri Tanzania nzima —
Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar pamoja. Hizi ni Mambo ya Muungano (22 kwa sasa).
Makao makuu ya Serikali ya Muungano ni Dodoma (mji mkuu) na Dar es Salaam
(mji mkuu wa kiuchumi).

Rais wa Muungano ndiye mkuu wa Serikali ya Muungano. Anachaguliwa na
wananchi wote wa Tanzania — Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar.

SERIKALI YA PILI: SERIKALI YA MAPINDUZI YA ZANZIBAR

Serikali ya Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar inasimamia mambo ya ndani ya Zanzibar
ambayo si sehemu ya Mambo ya Muungano. Makao yake ni Zanzibar Town.

Rais wa Zanzibar ndiye mkuu wa Serikali ya Mapinduzi. Anachaguliwa na
wananchi wa Zanzibar tu. Rais wa Zanzibar pia ni Makamu wa Pili wa Rais
wa Jamhuri ya Muungano.

TANZANIA BARA NA SERIKALI YA MUUNGANO

Tanzania Bara haijui serikali yake tofauti ya kipekee. Badala yake,
mambo ya Tanzania Bara yanasimamia na Serikali ya Muungano. Hii ni tofauti
na Zanzibar ambayo ina serikali yake ya ndani.

MADA INAYOENDELEA — SERIKALI TATU?

Kwa muda mrefu kumekuwa na mjadala kuhusu kama Tanzania inapaswa kuwa
na serikali tatu badala ya mbili:
- Serikali ya Muungano
- Serikali ya Tanzania Bara (mpya)
- Serikali ya Zanzibar (iliyopo)

Watetezi wa wazo hili wanasema Tanzania Bara inapaswa pia kuwa na serikali
yake ya ndani kama Zanzibar inavyo. Upande wa pili unasema serikali tatu
zingeweza kuwa na gharama kubwa na kufanya uratibu kuwa mgumu zaidi.
Mjadala huu bado unaendelea.

UNION STRUCTURE — TWO-GOVERNMENT SYSTEM

Tanzania's Union has a unique two-government structure — different from
most ordinary countries with one government. This structure is called
the "Two-Government Structure."

FIRST GOVERNMENT: GOVERNMENT OF THE UNITED REPUBLIC

The Union Government manages matters affecting all of Tanzania — Mainland
Tanzania and Zanzibar together. These are the Union Matters (currently 22).
The Union Government's headquarters are in Dodoma (capital) and Dar es
Salaam (economic capital).

The President of the Union is head of the Union Government. Elected by
all citizens of Tanzania — both Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.

SECOND GOVERNMENT: ZANZIBAR REVOLUTIONARY GOVERNMENT

The Zanzibar Revolutionary Government manages Zanzibar's internal affairs
that are not part of Union Matters. Its headquarters are in Zanzibar Town.

The President of Zanzibar is head of the Revolutionary Government. Elected
by Zanzibar citizens only. The President of Zanzibar is also the Second
Vice-President of the United Republic.

MAINLAND TANZANIA AND THE UNION GOVERNMENT

Mainland Tanzania does not have its own separate government. Instead,
Mainland Tanzania's affairs are managed by the Union Government. This is
different from Zanzibar which has its own internal government.

ONGOING DEBATE — THREE GOVERNMENTS?

For a long time there has been debate about whether Tanzania should have
three governments instead of two:
- Union Government
- Mainland Tanzania Government (new)
- Zanzibar Government (existing)

Proponents of this idea say Mainland Tanzania should also have its own
internal government like Zanzibar does. The opposing side says three
governments could be expensive and make coordination more difficult.
This debate is ongoing.
