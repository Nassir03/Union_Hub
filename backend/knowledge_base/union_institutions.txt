TAASISI ZA MUUNGANO WA TANZANIA

Taasisi za Muungano ni vyombo vinavyotekeleza kazi za serikali kwa ajili
ya Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar zote mbili. Zinasimamia mambo ya Muungano
na kuhakikisha taifa linafanya kazi kama moja.

TAASISI KUU ZA MUUNGANO

1. RAIS WA JAMHURI YA MUUNGANO
Rais ndiye mkuu wa nchi na mkuu wa serikali ya Muungano. Anachaguliwa
na wananchi moja kwa moja kwa kipindi cha miaka mitano (5). Rais ana
mamlaka ya kuteua mawaziri, majaji, na maafisa wakuu wa serikali.

2. BUNGE LA JAMHURI YA MUUNGANO
Bunge ni chombo cha kutunga sheria za Muungano. Lina wabunge kutoka
Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar. Mkutano mkuu wa Bunge hufanyika Dodoma,
mji mkuu wa Tanzania.

3. BARAZA LA MAWAZIRI (CABINET)
Baraza la Mawaziri linaloongozwa na Waziri Mkuu linasaidia Rais kutekeleza
sera za serikali ya Muungano. Mawaziri wanashughulikia wizara mbalimbali
kama afya, elimu, ulinzi, mambo ya nje, na kadhalika.

4. MAHAKAMA YA RUFANI YA TANZANIA
Mahakama ya Rufani ndiyo mahakama ya juu zaidi ya Muungano kwa kesi za
madai. Inashughulikia rufaa kutoka Mahakama ya Juu ya Tanzania na
Mahakama ya Juu ya Zanzibar (kwa baadhi ya kesi).

5. JESHI LA ULINZI WA WANANCHI WA TANZANIA (JWTZ)
JWTZ ni nguvu za ulinzi za Muungano. Linajumuisha jeshi la ardhini, jeshi
la anga, na jeshi la majini. Linajibika kwa usalama wa mipaka ya taifa
na ulinzi wa wananchi.

6. JESHI LA POLISI LA TANZANIA
Polisi wa Tanzania ni nguvu za usalama wa Muungano zinazohakikisha utaratibu
wa sheria Tanzania Bara. Zanzibar ina Jeshi lake la Polisi la Zanzibar.

7. TAASISI ZA FEDHA ZA MUUNGANO
- Benki Kuu ya Tanzania (BOT): Inasimamia sera ya fedha na sarafu
- Mamlaka ya Mapato Tanzania (TRA): Inakusanya kodi za Muungano
- Hazina ya Taifa: Inasimamia mapato na matumizi ya serikali

8. TAASISI ZA ELIMU YA JUU
Vyuo Vikuu vya Muungano ni pamoja na Chuo Kikuu cha Dar es Salaam
(UDSM), Chuo Kikuu cha Dodoma (UDOM), na Chuo Kikuu cha Sokoine cha
Kilimo (SUA). Vinakubali wanafunzi kutoka Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar.

TAASISI ZA SERIKALI YA ZANZIBAR

Ndani ya mfumo wa Muungano, Zanzibar ina taasisi zake:
- Rais wa Zanzibar na Baraza la Mapinduzi
- Baraza la Wawakilishi (Bunge la Zanzibar)
- Jeshi la Polisi la Zanzibar
- Mahakama ya Juu ya Zanzibar

UNION INSTITUTIONS OF TANZANIA

Union institutions are the bodies that carry out government functions
for both Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar. They manage Union Matters and
ensure the nation works as one.

KEY UNION INSTITUTIONS

1. PRESIDENT OF THE UNITED REPUBLIC
The President is both head of state and head of the Union Government.
Directly elected by citizens for a five-year (5) term. The President
has authority to appoint ministers, judges, and senior government officials.

2. PARLIAMENT OF THE UNITED REPUBLIC
Parliament is the body that makes Union laws. It includes MPs from both
Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar. Parliament's main sessions are held in
Dodoma, Tanzania's capital.

3. CABINET (BARAZA LA MAWAZIRI)
The Cabinet led by the Prime Minister assists the President in implementing
Union Government policies. Ministers manage various ministries such as
health, education, defence, foreign affairs, and so on.

4. COURT OF APPEAL OF TANZANIA
The Court of Appeal is the highest Union court for civil cases. It handles
appeals from the High Court of Tanzania and the High Court of Zanzibar
(for certain cases).

5. TANZANIA PEOPLE'S DEFENCE FORCES (TPDF/JWTZ)
TPDF is the Union's defence force. It includes the army, air force, and
navy. It is responsible for national border security and protection of
citizens.

6. TANZANIA POLICE FORCE
The Tanzania Police Force is the Union's security force ensuring law and
order in Mainland Tanzania. Zanzibar has its own Zanzibar Police Force.

7. UNION FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS
- Bank of Tanzania (BOT): Manages monetary policy and currency
- Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA): Collects Union taxes
- National Treasury: Manages government revenue and expenditure

8. HIGHER EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS
Union universities include the University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM),
University of Dodoma (UDOM), and Sokoine University of Agriculture (SUA).
They accept students from both Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.

ZANZIBAR GOVERNMENT INSTITUTIONS

Within the Union framework, Zanzibar has its own institutions:
- President of Zanzibar and the Revolutionary Council
- House of Representatives (Zanzibar's Parliament)
- Zanzibar Police Force
- High Court of Zanzibar
