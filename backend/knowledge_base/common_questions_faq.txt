======================================================================
KISWAHILI — MASWALI NA MAJIBU
======================================================================

S: Muungano ni nini?
J: Muungano wa Tanganyika na Zanzibar ni makubaliano ya kisiasa na kikatiba
ya mwaka 1964 ambayo yaliunda Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania. Uliundwa
tarehe 26 Aprili 1964, baada ya Hati za Muungano kusainiwa tarehe 22 Aprili
1964 na Julius Nyerere na Abeid Karume.

S: Muungano uliundwa lini?
J: Muungano uliundwa rasmi tarehe 26 Aprili 1964. Hati za Muungano
zilisainiwa tarehe 22 Aprili 1964.

S: Nani walitia saini Hati za Muungano?
J: Hati za Muungano zilisainiwa na Julius Kambarage Nyerere, Rais wa
Tanganyika, na Sheikh Abeid Amani Karume, Rais wa Zanzibar, tarehe
22 Aprili 1964.

S: Kwa nini Tanganyika na Zanzibar waliungana?
J: Sababu kuu ni pamoja na: Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar (Januari 1964) yaliyoleta
msukosuko, shinikizo la Vita Baridi, maadili ya Pan-Africanism, historia
na lugha ya pamoja (Kiswahili), na nguvu za pamoja na usalama.

S: Mambo ya Muungano ni yapi?
J: Kuna mambo 22 ya Muungano, ikiwemo: ulinzi, mambo ya nje, uraia,
polisi, benki kuu, elimu ya juu, takwimu, sarafu, bandari, posta na
mawasiliano, kazi, na mengine.

S: Tanganyika ilipata uhuru lini?
J: Tanganyika ilipata uhuru wake tarehe 9 Desemba 1961.

S: Zanzibar ilipata uhuru lini?
J: Zanzibar ilipata uhuru wake tarehe 10 Desemba 1963.

S: Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar yalifanyika lini?
J: Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar yalifanyika tarehe 12 Januari 1964.

S: Tanzania inapata jina lake vipi?
J: Jina "Tanzania" limechanganya maneno "Tanganyika" na "Zanzibar."
Jina hili lilianza kutumika rasmi tarehe 29 Oktoba 1964.

S: Mji mkuu wa Tanzania ni upi?
J: Dodoma ni mji mkuu wa Tanzania (wa serikali). Dar es Salaam ni mji
mkuu wa kiuchumi na kibiashara. Zanzibar Town (Mji Mkongwe) ni mji mkuu
wa Zanzibar.

======================================================================
ENGLISH — QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS
======================================================================

Q: What is the Union?
A: The Union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar is a political and constitutional
arrangement of 1964 that created the United Republic of Tanzania. It was
officially formed on 26 April 1964, after the Articles of Union were
signed on 22 April 1964 by Julius Nyerere and Abeid Karume.

Q: When was the Union formed?
A: The Union was officially formed on 26 April 1964. The Articles of
Union were signed on 22 April 1964.

Q: Who signed the Articles of Union?
A: The Articles of Union were signed by Julius Kambarage Nyerere, President
of Tanganyika, and Sheikh Abeid Amani Karume, President of Zanzibar, on
22 April 1964.

Q: Why did Tanganyika and Zanzibar unite?
A: The key reasons include: the Zanzibar Revolution (January 1964) which
brought instability, Cold War pressures, Pan-African ideals, shared history
and language (Swahili), and the need for combined strength and security.

Q: What are Union Matters?
A: There are 22 Union Matters, including: defence, foreign affairs,
citizenship, police, the central bank, higher education, statistics,
currency, ports, posts and telecommunications, labour, and others.

Q: When did Tanganyika gain independence?
A: Tanganyika gained independence on 9 December 1961.

Q: When did Zanzibar gain independence?
A: Zanzibar gained independence on 10 December 1963.

Q: When did the Zanzibar Revolution happen?
A: The Zanzibar Revolution took place on 12 January 1964.

Q: How did Tanzania get its name?
A: The name "Tanzania" is a combination of "Tanganyika" and "Zanzibar."
This name came into official use on 29 October 1964.

Q: What is Tanzania's capital city?
A: Dodoma is Tanzania's official capital (government seat). Dar es Salaam
is the economic and commercial capital. Zanzibar Town (Stone Town) is
Zanzibar's capital.
