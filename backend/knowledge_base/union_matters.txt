MAMBO YA MUUNGANO

Mambo ya Muungano ni orodha ya masuala yanayosimamiwa na Serikali ya
Jamhuri ya Muungano kwa ajili ya Tanzania Bara na Zanzibar zote mbili.
Kwa sasa kuna mambo 22 ya Muungano yaliyoainishwa katika Jedwali la Kwanza
la Katiba ya Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania.

Masuala yote yasiyokuwepo kwenye orodha hii yanabaki chini ya mamlaka ya
Serikali ya Mapinduzi ya Zanzibar kwa upande wa Zanzibar, au Serikali ya
Muungano kwa upande wa Tanzania Bara.

ORODHA YA MAMBO 22 YA MUUNGANO

1. Katiba na Serikali ya Jamhuri ya Muungano
2. Mambo ya Nje (Foreign Affairs)
3. Ulinzi na Usalama
4. Polisi
5. Mamlaka ya Dharura (Emergency Powers)
6. Uraia na Uhamiaji
7. Mahakama ya Rufani
8. Elimu ya Juu
9. Takwimu
10. Benki Kuu na Sarafu
11. Mikopo na Biashara ya Nje
12. Kodi ya Mapato na Ushuru wa Bidhaa
13. Bandari na Usafiri wa Baharini
14. Usafiri wa Anga (Reli, Barabara Kuu, na Viwanja vya Ndege)
15. Posta na Mawasiliano
16. Umeme (Nishati)
17. Kazi (Labour)
18. Utangazaji wa Habari za Umma (National Broadcasting)
19. Vyama vya Siasa
20. Mahakama ya Kesi (Court of Appeal)
21. Usalama wa Taifa
22. Mazingira (Environment) — iliongezwa baadaye

UMUHIMU WA KUELEWA MAMBO YA MUUNGANO

Kuelewa mambo ya Muungano kunasaidia raia kujua:
- Ni serikali gani inayosimamia suala fulani
- Ni wapi pa kwenda kutafuta huduma fulani ya serikali
- Jinsi Tanzania inavyofanya kazi kama taifa moja lenye mifumo miwili ya serikali
- Haki zao kama raia wa Tanzania na Zanzibar

Kwa vijana hasa, kuelewa mipaka hii ya mamlaka ni muhimu kwa kushiriki
kikamilifu katika mchakato wa demokrasia.

UNION MATTERS

Union Matters are the list of issues managed by the Government of the
United Republic of Tanzania for both Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.
Currently there are 22 Union Matters listed in the First Schedule of
the Constitution of the United Republic of Tanzania.

All matters not on this list remain under the authority of the Zanzibar
Revolutionary Government for Zanzibar's side, or the Union Government
for Mainland Tanzania.

LIST OF THE 22 UNION MATTERS

1. Constitution and Government of the United Republic
2. Foreign Affairs
3. Defence and Security
4. Police
5. Emergency Powers
6. Citizenship and Immigration
7. The Court of Appeal
8. Higher Education
9. Statistics
10. The Central Bank and Currency
11. External Borrowing and Trade
12. Income Tax and Customs Duties
13. Harbours and Shipping
14. Air Transport (Railways, Trunk Roads, and Airports)
15. Posts and Telecommunications
16. Electricity (Energy)
17. Labour
18. National Broadcasting Services
19. Political Parties
20. Court of Appeal
21. National Security
22. Environment — added later

WHY UNDERSTANDING UNION MATTERS IS IMPORTANT

Understanding Union Matters helps citizens know:
- Which government manages a particular issue
- Where to go to access a particular government service
- How Tanzania works as one nation with two government systems
- Their rights as citizens of Tanzania and Zanzibar

For youth especially, understanding these boundaries of authority is
vital for fully participating in the democratic process.
