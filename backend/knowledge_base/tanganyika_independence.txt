UHURU WA TANGANYIKA (9 DESEMBA 1961)

MANDHARI YA KIHISTORIA

Tanganyika ilikuwa chini ya utawala wa Ujerumani kama koloni (Deutsch-
Ostafrika) tangu miaka ya 1880 hadi Vita Kuu ya Kwanza ya Dunia (1914-1918).
Baada ya vita hiyo, Tanganyika ilikabidhiwa kwa Uingereza kwa njia ya
"mandate" ya Ligi ya Mataifa, na baadaye ikawa eneo la kuaminika la
Umoja wa Mataifa (UN Trust Territory) chini ya Uingereza.

HARAKATI ZA UHURU

Julius Kambarage Nyerere alianzisha Tanganyika African National Union
(TANU) tarehe 7 Julai 1954. TANU ilikuwa chama cha kisiasa kilichoundwa
kupigania uhuru wa Tanganyika kwa njia ya amani na mazungumzo. Nyerere
alikuwa na imani kwamba uhuru unaweza kupatikana bila vita.

TANU ilijenga msaada mkubwa kati ya wananchi wa vijijini na mjini kupitia
mikutano ya umma, uhamasishaji wa kisiasa, na mazungumzo na utawala wa
Uingereza. Shinikizo la kimataifa kutoka Umoja wa Mataifa pia lilisaidia
kuharakisha uhuru.

UHURU NA BAADAYE

Tarehe 9 Desemba 1961, Tanganyika ilipata uhuru wake. Julius Nyerere
akawa Waziri Mkuu wa kwanza wa Tanganyika huru.

Tarehe 9 Desemba 1962, Tanganyika iligeuzwa kuwa Jamhuri. Julius Nyerere
akawa Rais wa kwanza. Rashidi Mfaume Kawawa akawa Makamu wa Rais.

Baadaye, tarehe 26 Aprili 1964, Tanganyika iliungana na Zanzibar kuunda
Jamhuri ya Muungano wa Tanzania.

UMUHIMU WA UHURU WA TANGANYIKA

Tanganyika ilikuwa nchi ya kwanza katika Afrika Mashariki kupata uhuru.
Hii ilichochea harakati za uhuru katika nchi nyingine za Afrika Mashariki
na kwa Afrika nzima. Nyerere alikuwa mfano mkubwa wa kiongozi wa Afrika
aliyepigania uhuru kwa njia ya amani.

MCHANGO WA TANGANYIKA KATIKA MUUNGANO

Ukombozi wa amani wa Tanganyika ulitoa mfano na nguvu kwa Muungano.
Nyerere alimleta utulivu na hekima ya uongozi ambayo ilisaidia kuimarisha
msingi wa Muungano katika miaka yake ya awali.

TANGANYIKA INDEPENDENCE (9 DECEMBER 1961)

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

Tanganyika was under German colonial rule (Deutsch-Ostafrika) from the
1880s until World War One (1914-1918). After that war, Tanganyika was
handed to Britain under a League of Nations mandate, and later became
a United Nations Trust Territory under British administration.

THE INDEPENDENCE MOVEMENT

Julius Kambarage Nyerere founded the Tanganyika African National Union
(TANU) on 7 July 1954. TANU was a political party created to fight for
Tanganyika's independence through peaceful means and negotiation. Nyerere
believed independence could be achieved without war.

TANU built broad support among rural and urban citizens through public
meetings, political mobilisation, and negotiations with the British
administration. International pressure from the United Nations also
helped accelerate independence.

INDEPENDENCE AND AFTER

On 9 December 1961, Tanganyika gained independence. Julius Nyerere
became the first Prime Minister of independent Tanganyika.

On 9 December 1962, Tanganyika became a Republic. Julius Nyerere became
the first President. Rashidi Mfaume Kawawa became Vice-President.

Later, on 26 April 1964, Tanganyika merged with Zanzibar to form the
United Republic of Tanzania.

SIGNIFICANCE OF TANGANYIKA'S INDEPENDENCE

Tanganyika was the first country in East Africa to gain independence.
This inspired independence movements in other East African countries
and across Africa. Nyerere became a major example of an African leader
who fought for independence through peaceful means.

TANGANYIKA'S CONTRIBUTION TO THE UNION

Tanganyika's peaceful liberation provided a model and strength for the
Union. Nyerere brought stability and leadership wisdom that helped
strengthen the Union's foundation in its early years.
